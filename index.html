Hi, welcome to my personal page!

I obtained my Bachelor of Engineering from the University of Manchester in the United Kingdom and my Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering with a focus on high-tech engineering from Delft University of Technology.

My research interests centre on computational design and the use of computers to help engineers realise designs beyond traditional intuition. I am particularly interested in optimisation, especially structural and topology optimisation. This has led me to study related areas such as numerical modelling with an emphasis on finite element methods, continuum mechanics with a focus on solid mechanics, dynamics with a focus on vibrations, and control systems in combination with dynamics and mechatronics.

My long-term goal is to work in research and development or academia. I place high value on knowledge sharing and enjoy teaching, as well as creating clear and educational visual materials that make complex ideas easier to understand.

Outside of work, I enjoy rock climbing, meditation, cycling, running, and sailing. I have a strong appreciation for music and sound, as well as visual arts such as cinema, painting, drawing, and digital art. I am drawn to original and high-quality creative work. I am driven by continuous learning and improvement, and my guiding principle is simple: “It’s a marathon, not a sprint.”
